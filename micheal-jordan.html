Michael Jordan

Basketball Icon | 6× NBA Champion | Global Legend

Career Highlights

Michael Jordan, also known as "MJ," is widely regarded as the greatest basketball player of all time. Over a career that spanned more than a decade, he led the Chicago Bulls to six NBA championships and earned five MVP awards.

  • 6× NBA Champion (1991–1993, 1996–1998)
  • 5× NBA Most Valuable Player
  • 2× Olympic Gold Medalist
  • 14× NBA All-Star

Legacy

Jordan redefined what it means to be a global athlete. He made "Air Jordan" a household name, inspired generations of athletes, and elevated the NBA’s popularity around the world. His leadership, competitiveness, and winning mindset have made him a symbol of excellence both on and off the court.
